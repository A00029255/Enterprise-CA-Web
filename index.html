Density = Population / Area (people per km²)

Why Density

Population density is critical for urban planning, resource allocation, and understanding environmental impact, as it measures the concentration of individuals (people or species) within a specific area. High-density areas demand intense infrastructure, healthcare, and waste management, while low-density areas may face challenges with service delivery and population decline.
